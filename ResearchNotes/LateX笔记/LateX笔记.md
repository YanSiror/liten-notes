## 公式样式

|            | **符号** |    **表达式**    |
| :--------: | :------: | :--------------: |

## 换行与对齐

- **首字母对齐**
  $$
  \begin{aligned}
  &max=2*x1+3*x2;\\
  &x1+2*x2<=8;\\
  &4*x1<=16;\\
  &s4*x2<=12; 
  \end{aligned}
  $$
  **代码**

  ```
  $$
  \begin{aligned}
  &max=2*x1+3*x2;\\
  &x1+2*x2<=8;\\
  &4*x1<=16;\\
  &s4*x2<=12; 
  \end{aligned}
  $$
  ```

- **首个等号对齐**
  $$
  \begin{aligned}
  P(B|A)&=\frac{P(AB)}{P(A)}\\
  P(\overline{B}|A)&=1-P(B|A)=1-\frac{P(AB)}{P(A)}
  \end{aligned}
  $$
  **代码**

  ```
  $$
  \begin{aligned}
  P(B|A)&=\frac{P(AB)}{P(A)}\\
  P(\overline{B}|A)&=1-P(B|A)=1-\frac{P(AB)}{P(A)}
  \end{aligned}
  $$
  ```
